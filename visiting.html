Visitors
Visiting Hours & Policy Hospital Amenities

Havana Hospital visiting hours are every day from 9 a.m. to 10 p.m. However, if you or your family member is in Intensive Care, you should check for restrictions due to condition, scheduled tests or therapy. In those cases, you can contact the specific unit for visitor instructions. Call +254710566666 for more information.

Visiting Policies
  1. Families should check with the patient’s primary nurse before bringing children to visit a hospitalized patient.
  2. No more than two people visiting a patient at one time.
  3. Children under the age of 12 are only allowed to visit with the written permission of a physician and must be supervised by a parent or guardian at all times.
